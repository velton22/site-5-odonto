What is a waitlist template?

A waitlist template is a pre-designed webpage where users can sign up to join a waitlist for your product or service, helping you capture early interest and build anticipation.

Can I customize the waitlist template?

Yes, our Framer template is fully customizable. You can easily update the design, colors, text, and branding to suit your needs.

Does it integrate with email marketing tools?

Absolutely! The template supports integrations with popular email marketing tools like Mailchimp, HubSpot, and ConvertKit to manage your waitlist effectively.

Is the template mobile-friendly?

Yes, the template is fully responsive, ensuring a seamless user experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

How do I access the template after purchase?

Once purchased, you’ll receive a link to the Framer project, allowing you to duplicate and start customizing it instantly.
